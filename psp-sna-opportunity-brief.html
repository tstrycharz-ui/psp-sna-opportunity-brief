The most conservative measure. If we count only the revenue from a brand-new customer's very first booking and assume they never return, we'd need 192 new customers over the year to fully cover the $48,000 spend. This is the worst-case bar — any repeat business above this puts the placement in the black.

Annual Customer Value (ACV $750) — 64 customers won to cover a full year

A realistic mid-range view. The average PSP customer spends $750 across the year through multiple visits, not just one. At that rate, 64 new customers acquired through the placement covers the full annual cost. That's roughly 5–6 new clients per month — a practical benchmark that reflects normal repeat-visit behavior.

Lifetime Value (LTV $1,500) — 32 customers won to cover a full year

The most compelling view. Over the full life of a customer relationship, each new pet owner is worth $1,500 to PSP. At that measure, just 32 new clients over the course of a year — fewer than 3 per month — covers the entire annual spend. Every customer beyond that is pure margin. This is the number that captures the real return on acquiring a loyal, recurring customer.

Operational Notes
Attribution

QR codes are permitted with no restrictions. The QR code can be linked to the PSP location page (pawsweetpaw.com/dog-boarding-near-john-wayne-airport) and/or the online booking URL. A dedicated landing page would also allow scan-level tracking for the duration of the placement.

Creative approval timeline

All concepts must be approved by the airport before posting. Lead time should be factored into any planned start date.

Wi-Fi portal

Not available for this placement due to an existing contract with the airport's Wi-Fi provider.

Competitive context

The prior occupant (PetPoint Medical Center & Resort) ran a pet care campaign in this space before it ended. No pet care brand is currently active at SNA.

Summary

The placement is located in the only pet relief area in the airport, drawing from all three terminals. The space is currently vacant. A full-year commitment runs $48,000 ($4,000/month) — breakeven at 192 new customers on a first-visit basis, 64 on annual value, or just 32 on a $1,500 lifetime value basis (fewer than 3 per month). QR-based attribution is available, and the Jamboree location is 2.5 miles from SNA.
